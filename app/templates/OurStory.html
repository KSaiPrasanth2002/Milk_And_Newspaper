Our Story

Bringing simplicity back with our startup: natural, fresh milk delivered straight from local farms to your doorstep, ensuring purity and peace of mind. Say goodbye to reconstituted milk and adulteration worries, and hello to the freshness of the past.

Startup Milk & Newspaper, founded in 2024, comprises a team of innovative minds, blending tech expertise with a vision to revolutionize dairy and news consumption. Our pioneering mobile app streamlines milk and newspaper delivery, ensuring natural, fresh, and hassle-free service right at your doorstep.
